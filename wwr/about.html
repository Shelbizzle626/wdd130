History

River Rush Adventures was founded in 1987 by former Olympic kayaker Jake Morrison and his wife, outdoor educator Sarah Morrison. What started as weekend trips for friends on the Salmon River quickly grew into Idaho's premier white water rafting outfitter.

In our first season, we ran just 15 trips with two rafts. Today, River Rush Adventures operates daily excursions from May through September, employing over 40 certified river guides and maintaining a fleet of 25 state-of-the-art rafts.

Now in our fourth decade, River Rush Adventures remains family-owned and operated. The Morrisons' daughter, Emma, serves as Lead Guide Instructor, ensuring every guest experiences the same authentic rush that started it all.
